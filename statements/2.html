Hello there! We have got a new problem for solving! it's also a problem with checker. Your task is to output a permutation of the numbers 1 to N (1<N<1000) in which the number on index(indexation of an array begins in 0!!!) a is b (0<a, b<N). Of course the task of the checker is harder but that isn't on your mind!

Input
On the first line of the standard input will be the numbers N, a and b.

Output
On the first line of the standard output you have to print any correct permutation.

Example Input 1:
5 3 2

Possible Output for Example 1:
5 3 1 2 4
